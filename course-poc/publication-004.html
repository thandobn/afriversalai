Part 1
Welcome

1. Welcome to AfriversalAI

Thank you for choosing AfriversalAI as your enterprise AI capability partner. Artificial intelligence is transforming how organisations work, make decisions, serve customers and develop their people. Successful AI adoption requires more than access to technology — it requires informed leadership, capable employees and responsible governance. We look forward to partnering with your organisation to build practical AI capability that supports your strategic objectives.

2. About Your Programme

Your organisation has enrolled in an enterprise learning programme designed to help employees understand AI, evaluate AI outputs critically, apply AI responsibly, strengthen professional judgement and support organisational AI readiness. Your programme may include self-paced online learning, live facilitator-led sessions, practical workplace activities, assessments, digital certification and executive reporting.

Part 2
Your Project

4. Your Implementation Team

Customer: Executive Sponsor; Programme Administrator; HR / Learning Representative; IT Representative (if required); Department Managers. AfriversalAI: Customer Success Manager; Project Manager; Learning Success Lead; Lead Facilitator; Certification Administrator.

5. Roles & Responsibilities

Executive Sponsor — champions the programme, removes barriers, reviews outcomes, supports adoption. Programme Administrator — coordinates enrolment, maintains learner lists, communicates with participants, primary customer contact. AfriversalAI Customer Success Manager — oversees implementation, coordinates delivery, monitors progress, provides executive updates, supports continuous improvement.

Part 3
Programme Delivery

7. Learner Registration

Your Programme Administrator submits the approved learner list, confirms participant details, reviews seat allocations and validates departments. AfriversalAI issues Access Codes after the agreed commercial requirements have been satisfied.

8. Live Sessions

Depending on your programme, live sessions may include the Executive Launch Session, AI ThinkLab™, Governance Forum™, Strategy Lab™ and Ask-the-Expert sessions. Attendance requirements are communicated in advance.

9. Assessments & Certification

Successful learners receive a digital certificate, a unique Member ID and a certificate verification record. Certification requirements are described in Publication AAI-P016 (Certification, Assessment & Credentialing Policy).

Part 4
Customer Success

10. Reporting

Your organisation may receive reports including enrolment status, learner participation, completion rates, assessment results, certification summary, executive dashboard and programme recommendations. Report frequency is defined in the Statement of Work.

11. Customer Support

AfriversalAI provides support for learner access, password assistance, registration queries, programme administration, certification enquiries and reporting requests. Support arrangements are governed by Publication AAI-P009 (Enterprise Service Level Agreement).

12. Escalation

If an issue cannot be resolved through normal support channels: 1) Customer Success Manager → 2) Project Manager → 3) Head of Enterprise Delivery → 4) Executive Sponsor.

Part 5
Next Steps

13. Programme Completion

At completion, your organisation receives an Executive Completion Report, Learner Completion Report, Certification Summary, recommendations for future learning pathways and a Benefits Realisation review.

14. Continuing Your AI Journey

Completion represents the beginning of your organisation’s AI capability journey. AfriversalAI can support continued development through Advanced AI Literacy Programmes, Executive AI Leadership, AI Governance, AI Policy Development, department-specific learning pathways and workforce transformation consulting.
