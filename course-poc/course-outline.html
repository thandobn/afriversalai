Professional Certification Framework · Version 2.0

Africa's Professional Standards Institute for the Age of AI

A four-layer credentialing framework for South African professionals — built on real SA case studies, assessed through real-world tasks, and designed for judgment, not button-pressing.

Institutional positioning: AfriversalAI is not a training company. We are building the professional standards infrastructure that South Africa's AI workforce will be accredited against — the same role that SAICA plays for accounting, or the Law Society plays for legal practice.

The Framework

Every learner enters at Layer 1. From there, they branch into the credential path most relevant to their role and ambition. Each layer builds on the one below it.

1
Core Certification — Certified AI-Ready Professional™
Prerequisite for all layers · 7-week cohort · All sectors · All professionals
Entry credential

Eight modules built around South African AI case studies. Learners leave with the Funda Five framework and a demonstrated ability to evaluate any AI situation at work. This credential is the gateway to all other layers.
