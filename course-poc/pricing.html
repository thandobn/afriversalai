Frequently Asked Questions

Pricing questions

When will pricing be confirmed?
Pricing will be confirmed ahead of our first cohort opening. Register your interest and you'll be the first to know — before we open publicly.
Can my employer pay for the course?
Yes. We issue invoices to organisations, provide L&D reporting documentation, and once SETA accreditation is in place, your employer can recover 40–60% of the cost through the SDL discretionary grant system. Use the team registration form and we'll handle the rest.
Is there a discount for large groups?
Yes — volume discounts apply for organisations enrolling 10 or more learners. Contact us or use the team registration form and we'll include volume pricing in the proposal we send back.
What if I don't pass the capstone?
You get one revision opportunity. Your assessor will give you specific, written feedback on which criteria you need to address. If you don't meet the standard after revision, you can re-attempt in the next cohort — we'll work out a fair arrangement for returning learners.
Is there a free trial or preview?
Yes — Module 1, Phase 1 is free to preview right now. You'll encounter the core document, reflect on it, and experience how the course feels before committing to anything. No account required.
