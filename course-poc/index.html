The Problem

AI is already in South African workplaces.
The judgment skills aren't.

In April 2026, South Africa withdrew its national AI policy after journalists found it contained fabricated academic citations — generated by AI. The professionals who wrote it knew how to use AI tools. They didn't know when to question them. That gap is everywhere.

The Core Framework

The Funda Five

Every learner leaves with one framework they can apply to any AI encounter at work — in 2 minutes, without technical knowledge.

1
Task
Is this the right kind of job for AI?
2
Data
Is any of this sensitive or POPIA-protected?
3
Tool
Is this AI type approved by my organisation?
4
Trust
How would I detect an error in this output?
5
Human
Who checks this? Who is accountable if it's wrong?
