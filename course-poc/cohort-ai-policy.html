📋 AI Policy Cohort

Write an AI policy your organisation can actually stand behind.

Certified AI Policy Practitioner™

A guided, six-week intensive for the professionals who are handed the task of "sorting out our AI policy" — and given no map. You will leave with a complete, board-ready AI policy written for your organisation, an implementation plan to roll it out, and the judgment to defend every clause in it.

For: Policy analysts · Government advisors · Researchers · Advocacy professionals · Civil servants · Compliance & risk leads

The problem we're fixing. Right now, capable professionals are being told to "write the AI policy" with no framework, no precedent, and no time. So they paste the brief into ChatGPT and ship a document they can't explain, can't defend, and can't implement. A policy nobody understands is worse than no policy — it creates the illusion of governance. This cohort replaces that with real understanding and a real, workable document.

What you'll walk away with

This is not a lecture series. Every week you build one more part of a real AI policy for your own organisation. By the final week you have the finished article — not a certificate that says you watched some videos.

A complete AI policy for your organisation — scope, principles, roles, acceptable use, oversight, procurement and incident response.
A 12-month implementation roadmap — how to get it approved, adopted, trained and monitored.
The judgment to defend it — you can explain every clause, the law behind it, and the trade-offs you made.
A reusable toolkit — templates for the policy, risk register, impact assessment and vendor evaluation.
Command of the landscape — SA instruments and the international frameworks (EU AI Act, AU, OECD, NIST) that shape them.
The Certified AI Policy Practitioner™ credential — assessed by a domain expert against a published rubric.

Built on the Funda Five™ — not on guesswork

A good AI policy answers the same five questions our learners ask of every AI decision — just at the level of a whole organisation. The framework you already trust becomes the backbone of the document you write.

1 · Task
Which uses of AI is this policy actually governing? Scoping the policy to real decisions and real harm, not vague "AI usage".
2 · Data
POPIA, data classification, lawful basis, and what may never be put into a tool. The clauses that keep you out of court.
3 · Tool
Approved vs prohibited tools, procurement standards, and how to evaluate a vendor's AI claims before you sign.
4 · Trust
Risk classification, verification standards, monitoring and audit — how the organisation knows the policy is working.
5 · Human
Accountability lines, human-oversight requirements, sign-off and incident response. Who answers when it goes wrong.

The six weeks — how we hold your hand through it

Each week has a focus, the Funda Five™ lens it sharpens, a live faculty session, and one concrete thing you build toward your own policy. Nothing is left as "go figure it out".

Focus

Funda Five™

Focus
