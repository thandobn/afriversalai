1. Definitions

“Programme” means AfriversalAI’s AI-literacy training programme. “Learners” means the Customer’s employees enrolled under this Agreement. “Access Code” means the unique enrolment code issued for the Customer’s cohort. “Fees” means the amounts payable as set out in clause 4. “POPIA” means the Protection of Personal Information Act 4 of 2013.

2. Appointment & Services

The Customer appoints AfriversalAI to deliver the Programme to its Learners, and AfriversalAI accepts that appointment on the terms of this Agreement. AfriversalAI will provide access to the online Programme and, where specified, live facilitated cohort sessions, for the following:

  • Cohort / intake: [Intake label]
  • Number of seats: [Seats]
  • Price per seat (excl. VAT): R [Price per seat]
  • Sector: [Sector]

3. Access Codes & Enrolment

On confirmation of payment, AfriversalAI issues the Customer a unique Access Code. Each Learner registers with the Access Code, is grouped into the Customer’s cohort, and is issued a cohort-stamped Member ID that appears on their certificate. The number of enrolments may not exceed the number of seats purchased without a further order.

4. Fees & Payment

The Customer will pay the Fees set out in the tax invoice issued by AfriversalAI. Fees are exclusive of VAT, which is charged at the prevailing rate. Payment is made by electronic funds transfer to AfriversalAI’s nominated account, using the invoice number as reference. Access Codes and enrolment are released only once cleared funds are received. The Customer will upload proof of payment to its Corporate Portal.

5. Term

This Agreement commences on the Effective Date and continues for the duration of the cohort and any agreed support period, unless terminated earlier in accordance with its terms.

6. Intellectual Property

All content, materials, methodologies and the Funda Five framework comprising the Programme are and remain the exclusive property of AfriversalAI. The Customer and its Learners receive a limited, non-exclusive, non-transferable licence to access the Programme for internal training purposes only. No content may be copied, resold, sublicensed or used to build a competing offering.

7. Confidentiality

Each party will keep confidential the non-public information of the other disclosed under this Agreement and use it only for the purposes of this Agreement, save where disclosure is required by law.

8. Data Protection (POPIA)

Each party will comply with POPIA in respect of personal information processed under this Agreement. AfriversalAI processes Learner personal information to deliver the Programme, issue Member IDs and certificates, and report progress to the Customer’s nominated representative. The Customer confirms it has a lawful basis to share Learner details for enrolment.

9. Warranties

AfriversalAI will deliver the Programme with reasonable skill and care. The Customer warrants that the information it provides (including Learner details and its own registration particulars) is accurate and that its signatory is authorised to bind it.

10. Limitation of Liability

To the maximum extent permitted by law, neither party is liable for indirect or consequential loss. AfriversalAI’s total liability under this Agreement is limited to the Fees paid by the Customer in respect of the affected cohort.

11. Termination

Either party may terminate on written notice if the other commits a material breach that is not remedied within 14 days of notice. Fees for Access Codes already issued are non-refundable.

12. General

This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties and is governed by the laws of the Republic of South Africa. No variation is effective unless recorded in writing. If any provision is unenforceable, the remainder continues in effect.

Execution

By signing below, the parties agree to be bound by this Agreement. Signing electronically is legally binding under the Electronic Communications and Transactions Act 25 of 2002.
