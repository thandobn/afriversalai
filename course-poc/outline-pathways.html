Layer 2 — Sector Specializations

After completing the Layer 1 Core Certification, learners can pursue a sector-named practitioner credential in their industry. Each track goes deeper into sector-specific AI tools, applicable SA legislation, regulatory bodies, and real cases from that industry. The Layer 1 Module 7 sector case study is the foundation that Layer 2 builds on.

Layer 4 — Executive Programs

Intensive programs for executives and board members responsible for AI strategy at an organisational level. Cohort-based with peer learning, expert panels, and a strategic AI readiness assessment deliverable. Small cohorts (max 15 learners). Two tracks:

AI Leadership Program
For: CEOs, COOs, CIOs, CTOs, and senior executives setting AI strategy
  • AI strategy design and organisational readiness assessment
  • Leading cultural change in AI adoption
  • Evaluating AI vendor proposals and build-vs-buy decisions
  • Communicating AI risk to boards, investors, and regulators
  • Capstone: strategic AI readiness plan for their organisation
AI Board Governance Program
For: Non-executive directors, trustees, and board chairs
  • Director duties and AI risk in the SA regulatory context
  • Questioning management AI recommendations effectively
  • AI audit committee structures and oversight protocols
  • Scenario-based board simulation: AI crisis response
  • Capstone: AI governance policy for board adoption
Prerequisite: Layer 1 Core Certification or demonstrated equivalent competency assessed on application. Executive programs in design phase — target launch Q1 2027.
