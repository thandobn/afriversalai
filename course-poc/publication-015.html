Part A
Getting Started

1. Welcome

Welcome to AfriversalAI. You are joining a programme designed to help professionals develop the knowledge, judgement and confidence needed to work effectively with artificial intelligence. Success depends not only on understanding the technology, but on applying it responsibly, ethically and thoughtfully — this programme helps you build those capabilities.

2. About AfriversalAI

AfriversalAI partners with organisations across Africa and beyond to develop AI capability within the workforce, combining practical learning, critical thinking, responsible AI principles and workplace application to prepare individuals and organisations for the future of work.

3. Your Learning Journey

Online modulesInteractive activitiesWorkplace exercisesLive sessionsGroup discussionAssessmentsCertification

4. Programme Overview

Your organisation has enrolled you in a structured learning journey. The specific modules, timetable and live sessions will be communicated by your Programme Administrator.

Part B
Your Learning Experience

5. How Learning Works

You progress through modules in sequence. Modules may include videos, readings, knowledge checks, case studies, reflective exercises and practical activities. Complete each module before progressing unless instructed otherwise.

6. Live Sessions

Depending on your programme you may join the Live Programme Launch, AI ThinkLab™, Governance Forum™, Strategy Lab™ and Executive Q&A sessions. Attendance at certain live sessions may be mandatory for certification.

7. Assessments

Assessments help you demonstrate understanding and practical application, and may include quizzes, case studies, workplace scenarios, practical exercises and reflective activities. Follow all instructions carefully.

8. Certification

On successful completion, eligible learners receive a digital certificate, a unique AfriversalAI Member ID, and access to certificate verification. Requirements are described in Publication AAI-P016.

Part C
Expectations

9. Learner Responsibilities

Participate actively; complete required activities; attend mandatory live sessions; complete assessments honestly; protect your login credentials; and contact your Programme Administrator if you encounter difficulties.

10. Responsible Use of AI

When using AI tools: verify outputs before relying on them; apply independent judgement; protect confidential information; follow your organisation’s AI policies; and acknowledge AI assistance where required. AI can make mistakes — human judgement remains essential.

11. Academic Integrity

Your work should reflect your own understanding. Do not submit another person’s work as your own, share assessment answers, misuse AI contrary to instructions, or impersonate another learner. Integrity protects the value of your certification.

12. Professional Conduct

Treat others respectfully, contribute constructively during live sessions, maintain confidentiality where required, and behave professionally in all programme activities.

Part D
Support

13. Getting Help

For organisational questions, contact your Programme Administrator. For technical, assessment or certification queries, contact AfriversalAI through the agreed support channels. Support is provided per the Service Level Agreement (Publication 009).

14. Frequently Asked Questions

Common topics: logging in; accessing learning materials; live-session attendance; assessment requirements; certificates; technical support.

15. Continuing Your Learning Journey

Completing this programme is the beginning of your AI capability journey. Future opportunities may include advanced AI literacy programmes, AI governance, AI leadership, industry-specific pathways and executive programmes. Discuss options with your Programme Administrator or manager.
