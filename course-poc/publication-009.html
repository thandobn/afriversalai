Part A
Introduction

1. Purpose

This Service Level Agreement (“SLA”) describes the operational service commitments provided by AfriversalAI in support of the Services delivered under the Enterprise Master Services Agreement. It establishes measurable service expectations, support arrangements, communication processes and performance standards to help ensure successful delivery of the Programme.

2. Scope

This SLA applies to: Corporate Portal support; learner administration; programme administration; Access Code management; live virtual sessions; learning-platform support; reporting services; certification administration; and Customer Success services.

3. Definitions

Capitalised terms have the meanings given in the Master Services Agreement (Publication 010). “Business Hour/Day” means published support hours on a South African Business Day.

Part B
Service Delivery

4. Service Availability

Target availability: 99.5% during each calendar month, excluding scheduled maintenance and events outside AfriversalAI’s reasonable control.

Scheduled maintenance will, where practicable, be communicated in advance and planned to minimise disruption.

5. Support Services

AfriversalAI provides support for learner access, password resets, Access Code queries, Programme Administrator assistance, reporting requests, certification enquiries, and technical guidance relating to the learning platform — during published business support hours unless an enhanced support package has been agreed.

6. Customer Success Services

The Customer Success team may provide implementation guidance, onboarding assistance, progress monitoring, learner-engagement support, programme health reviews, executive reporting, and recommendations for continued AI capability development.

7. Programme Administration

AfriversalAI will configure approved programmes; issue Access Codes following confirmed payment; maintain learner records; administer assessments; issue digital certificates; and maintain Member ID records.

11. Escalation Procedure

Level 1: Customer Success Representative → Level 2: Project Manager → Level 3: Head of Delivery → Level 4: Executive Sponsor (AfriversalAI). Each escalation includes the issue description, actions taken, current status and proposed next steps.

Part D
Operational Services

12. Platform Maintenance

Scheduled maintenance may include software updates, security patches, infrastructure maintenance and feature enhancements. Where practicable, Customers are notified in advance. Emergency maintenance may be performed without prior notice to protect platform integrity or security.

13. Reporting

Standard reports may include learner enrolment, attendance, progress, assessment outcomes, certification status and executive summary. Frequency is defined in the Commercial Schedule or Statement of Work.

14. Communication

AfriversalAI maintains appropriate communication including implementation updates, incident notifications (where appropriate), scheduled service reviews, project progress reports and executive summaries.

15. Service Reviews

For larger enterprise engagements, periodic service reviews may cover programme progress, service performance, learner engagement, risks, issues and improvement opportunities, at an agreed frequency.

Part E
Governance

16. Service Exclusions

Unless specifically included in the Commercial Schedule, this SLA does not cover customer network infrastructure, third-party internet connectivity, customer-owned hardware, unsupported browsers/devices, bespoke software development, or third-party applications not supplied by AfriversalAI.

17. Customer Responsibilities

The Customer will appoint a Programme Administrator, provide accurate learner information, maintain appropriate internet connectivity, ensure learners comply with the Acceptable Use Policy, report issues promptly and cooperate in resolving incidents.

18. Continuous Improvement

AfriversalAI may periodically update learning content, delivery methods, support processes, reporting, technology platforms and operational procedures. Material changes affecting the Services will be communicated where appropriate.
