Privacy

Privacy Policy

How AfriversalAI collects, uses, and protects your personal information — in compliance with the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA), Act 4 of 2013.

POPIA Compliant · Last updated June 2026

1. Who we are

AfriversalAI is an AI literacy training initiative operated by its founders (the Responsible Party under POPIA). We offer AI literacy courses and training programmes for South African professionals and organisations.

Contact: afriversalai@gmail.com

2. What personal information we collect

When you submit a registration or contact form on this site, we collect the personal information you provide. This may include:

  • Full name
  • Work email address
  • Job title and professional role
  • Employer or organisation name
  • Industry sector
  • City or province of residence
  • Learning goals or course expectations (free-text fields)
  • How you heard about AfriversalAI

We do not collect payment information directly. We do not collect ID numbers, passport details, or sensitive personal information (as defined in POPIA Section 26) unless required for SETA assessment and accreditation purposes, in which case this will be collected separately with your explicit consent.

3. Why we collect your information

We collect personal information for the following purposes:

  • To follow up on your course registration or enquiry
  • To send you cohort dates, pricing, and onboarding information
  • To understand which sectors and regions are most interested in our training
  • To improve our course content and delivery
  • To comply with SETA accreditation requirements once accreditation is in place

We will not use your information for unsolicited marketing or share it with third parties for their marketing purposes.

4. How your information is processed

Form submissions

Our contact and registration forms are powered by Formspree (Formspree Inc.), a third-party form processing service. When you submit a form, your data is transmitted to and temporarily stored on Formspree's servers, which are located in the United States.

Cross-border transfer notice (POPIA Section 72): By submitting a form on this site, you acknowledge that your personal information will be transferred to the United States for processing. Formspree's privacy policy is available at formspree.io/legal/privacy-policy. We have determined that Formspree maintains reasonable data protection standards equivalent to POPIA requirements.

Email

Your submitted information is forwarded to AfriversalAI's email inbox and may be stored in our email system. We use standard email security practices to protect this data.

5. How long we keep your information

We retain your contact and registration information for a maximum of 24 months from the date of submission, or until you request deletion — whichever comes first. If you enrol in a course, we retain your assessment records for 5 years for certification purposes.

6. Your rights under POPIA

As a data subject under POPIA, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Delete your personal information (subject to legal and contractual requirements)
  • Object to the processing of your personal information
  • Complain to the Information Regulator of South Africa if you believe your rights have been violated

To exercise any of these rights, email us at afriversalai@gmail.com. We will respond within 10 business days.

Information Regulator (South Africa):
Website: www.justice.gov.za/inforeg
Email: inforeg@justice.gov.za

7. Cookies and tracking

This site is hosted on GitHub Pages and does not set any tracking cookies. We use Google Fonts, which loads fonts from Google's servers and may log your IP address as part of the font delivery request. We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any behavioural tracking tools.

8. Security

We take reasonable technical precautions to protect your personal information, including HTTPS encryption on all pages and spam filtering on all forms. We cannot guarantee absolute security of data transmitted over the internet.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as our services evolve. The date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. Material changes will be communicated to registered learners via email.

10. Contact us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or how we handle your personal information, contact us at:
afriversalai@gmail.com
