1. Purpose

This Agreement establishes the framework under which the Partner may introduce, promote, refer or support the sale and delivery of AfriversalAI products and services, creating long-term commercial relationships while protecting customers, intellectual property and the AfriversalAI brand.

2. Partner Categories

AfriversalAI may appoint Partners under one or more categories: Referral Partner; Sales Partner; Reseller (where expressly authorised); Strategic Alliance Partner; Implementation Partner; Education Partner; Government Partner; Industry Association Partner. The applicable category and any additional rights are recorded in the Partner Commercial Schedule.

3. Non-Exclusive Appointment

Unless expressly agreed in writing, the appointment is non-exclusive. AfriversalAI may appoint other partners, sell directly, or engage other representatives within the same territory or market.

4. Guiding Principles

Partners extend AfriversalAI’s reach; AfriversalAI remains the contracting supplier and owner of the customer relationship, learner records and intellectual property.

Part B
Commercial Model

5. Referral Opportunities

Partners may identify and introduce prospective customers. AfriversalAI retains sole discretion regarding whether to pursue an opportunity, pricing, commercial negotiations, contract approval, acceptance of purchase orders and programme delivery.

6. Sales Process

  1. Partner introduces opportunity.
  2. AfriversalAI qualifies the opportunity.
  3. Joint discovery meeting.
  4. Proposal issued by AfriversalAI.
  5. Commercial negotiation.
  6. Contract execution.
  7. Delivery by AfriversalAI.
  8. Commission paid in accordance with this Agreement.

7. Commission

Commission structures are defined in the Partner Commercial Schedule and may include Referral Commission, Implementation Commission, Renewal Commission and Performance Incentives. Commission becomes payable only after the Customer has paid AfriversalAI in accordance with the applicable invoice terms (and, per programme policy, within 15 days of confirmed and cleared funds).

Part C
Customer Ownership

8. Customer Relationship

Unless expressly agreed otherwise in writing: all customers contracted under AfriversalAI products or services remain customers of AfriversalAI; customer agreements are between AfriversalAI and the Customer; learner records, Member IDs, certification history, assessment records and programme data remain part of the AfriversalAI ecosystem; and the Partner does not acquire ownership of the customer relationship through referral alone.

9. Account Management

AfriversalAI may invite the Partner to participate in customer meetings, implementation or account planning where appropriate. Primary responsibility for contractual delivery, certification, quality assurance and governance remains with AfriversalAI unless otherwise agreed.

10. Renewals & Expansion

Unless agreed otherwise in writing: renewal discussions are managed by AfriversalAI; expansion opportunities remain opportunities for AfriversalAI; and commission eligibility on renewals is determined per the Commercial Schedule.

Part D
Partner Responsibilities

11. Sales & Conduct Standards

The Partner will act professionally and ethically; represent AfriversalAI accurately; use only approved marketing material; avoid misleading claims; comply with applicable laws; maintain confidentiality; protect customer information; and refer implementation questions to AfriversalAI where appropriate.

12. Brand Usage

The Partner may use approved AfriversalAI branding only in accordance with Publication AAI-P017 (Intellectual Property, Brand & Innovation Policy) and any published Brand Standards.

Part E
Protection

13. Intellectual Property

All intellectual property remains the exclusive property of AfriversalAI. Nothing transfers ownership of curriculum, methodologies, software, learning content, trademarks, publications, assessments or certification systems.

14. Non-Circumvention

Neither Party shall knowingly circumvent the other in relation to opportunities introduced under this Agreement. The Partner shall not seek to replace AfriversalAI as the supplier of AfriversalAI-branded services to a referred customer without AfriversalAI’s prior written consent.

15. Non-Solicitation

During the Agreement and for twenty-four (24) months after termination, the Partner shall not knowingly solicit for employment or engagement any AfriversalAI employee, facilitator, consultant or contractor directly involved in delivering the Services, unless agreed in writing.

16. Competition & Restrictions

Partners may offer other products and services provided they do not represent competing products as AfriversalAI offerings, misuse confidential information, infringe intellectual property, or create customer confusion regarding the source of services. Any exclusivity arrangements must be expressly documented.

Part F
General

17. Reporting

Partners may receive reports covering referred opportunities, pipeline status, closed business, commissions earned, commission payments and customer engagement activities, depending on category and Commercial Schedule.

18. Performance Reviews

AfriversalAI may conduct periodic business reviews covering customer satisfaction, revenue performance, pipeline development, compliance, marketing activities and strategic planning.

19. Term & Termination

This Agreement continues until terminated in accordance with its terms. Either Party may terminate for material breach not remedied within the agreed cure period or for other grounds set out herein. Termination does not affect accrued rights or obligations.

20. Effect of Termination

Following termination: outstanding commissions earned before termination remain payable per the Commercial Schedule; confidential information must be returned or destroyed where appropriate; use of AfriversalAI branding must cease; and customer contracts with AfriversalAI remain in force unless otherwise agreed.

21. Dispute Resolution & Governing Law

Disputes are escalated to senior executives, then mediation and arbitration in Johannesburg. This Agreement is governed by the laws of the Republic of South Africa.
