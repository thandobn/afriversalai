## Track Decision Gate

**The 4-track decision is deferred until Field 0 is complete.**

Field 0 will produce a ranked sector shortlist answering: *which ONE sector has the fastest path from warm conversation to signed pilot contract?*

That sector becomes Track 1. The other 3 tracks are prioritized after Track 1 is validated.

---

## Critical Path

Field 1 (primary interviews) is the gate for the ADDIE Design phase and is entirely in Thando's hands. All desk research (Fields 0, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6) should be completed while Field 1 interviews are being arranged and conducted.

**Field 0 and Field 6 are time-bounded 2-week sprints.** They run first and gate subsequent work.

---

## Definition of Done — Research Phase Complete When:

- [ ] Field 0: Sector shortlist produced, track 1 decision made
- [ ] Field 1: 5+ interviews completed and synthesized into validated persona
- [ ] Field 2: ≥10 SA-specific AI case studies documented with sector, what happened, lesson
- [ ] Field 3: Competency framework with ≥15 outcomes at Bloom's Apply/Evaluate, reviewed against real learner profile
- [ ] Field 4: SA buyer map with ≥3 named pilot candidate organizations; B-BBEE/SETA mechanics documented; founder credibility narrative drafted
- [ ] Field 5: Pedagogical blueprint with activity structure per module type; assessment rubric for AI judgment competency
- [ ] Field 6: SA competitor analysis complete for ≥4 providers with pricing, client sectors, and gap map
